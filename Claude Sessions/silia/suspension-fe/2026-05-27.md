### 6. Role-Based Suspension Access Control (v2)
- Replaced boolean isSuspended with suspensionMode: 'none' | 'limited' | 'blocked'

#### Superuser (role=superuser) → suspensionMode='none'
- Full bypass, no restrictions, no banner

#### Admin (role=admin) → suspensionMode='limited'
- Red banner shown
- Accessible: Billing (/settings/account), Data View (read-only), Performance/Metrics (read-only)
- Blocked (disabled + route guard): Home, Organization, Client Manager, Analytics, New Agent, Inbox, Knowledge, Workflows, Channels, Settings
- RequireActiveAccount redirects to /settings/account (billing)

#### User/Supervisor (role=user) → suspensionMode='blocked'
- Full-screen block page: 'Account Suspended — Contact your administrator' + logout button
- No sidebar, no navigation, no outlet
